FAQ


WHO IS MY INFORMATION SHARED WITH

Your information is only shared with The Bayesian Conspiracy ("TBC"). TBC does not have any partnership or other relationship with any government or private entity providing the benefit programs. TBC does not share your information with them and does not store user data for longer than is necessary to provide the service.

HOW WILL I KNOW IF I'M APPROVED

Bravo Six does not make an absolute determination of your eligibility for a program; you will need to follow the normal application procedures in order to actually apply, though it may help to have the Eligibility Results we generate nearby to help guide you through some of the criteria. For any questions about benefit approval, contact the appropriate agency.

WHEN CAN I EXPECT A RESPONSE

You can expect a response from Bravo Six about your potential eligibility immediately in the form of an Eligibility Results summary for various programs. However, these results do not constitute actual approval or denial for a program, and cannot account for all factors used in eligibility determination. For questions about receiving benefits through a program, contact the appropriate agency which adminsters the program.
